
Component-Based Architecture

1 What is a “component”?

A component is a self-contained unit that includes code ex.. html js  & css (structure , behaviour, style)
A Button, A music player widget  , a nav bar , a user profile card

2 What are the characteristics of a component?

A component isn’t just “a piece of code”—it has some specific traits that make it powerful in component-based architecture.
Reusable, self contained, has 1 job, works well with others, replaceable, 

3 What are the advantages of using component-based architecture?

Reusability , Easier Maintenance , Cleaner code organization
